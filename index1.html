Kusanagi is a community dedicated to anime and gaming. It was founded by three Dominaes: Eren Yeager, the Dominae Imperatix; Lord Grim, the Dominae Asmodeus; and Light Yagami, the Dominae Praefecta. "Kusanagi" is the name of a legendary sword in Japanese mythology. It is also associated with the mythical deity Susanoo, who is said to have found the sword in the tail of an eight-headed serpent.
